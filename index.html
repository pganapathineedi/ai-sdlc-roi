The opportunity

Traditional SDLC is labour-intensive and slow to adapt. This model quantifies AI savings and the true all-in cost — tokens, SaaS licences, Salesforce add-ons, infrastructure, and training — so the net benefit is fully defensible. Use the 💰 Cost model and 📐 Workings tabs to tailor every input to your client.

Where AI wins most

🏆 Development — up to 45% effort reduction
🏆 Testing / QA — up to 40% effort reduction
🏆 Documentation — up to 50% effort reduction
📊 Requirements — up to 25% effort reduction
📊 Monitoring — up to 25% effort reduction

Key cost risks

⚠️ Token costs scale with usage — monitor closely
⚠️ Agentforce priced per conversation, not per seat
⚠️ SF licence bundles can obscure true AI add-on cost
⚠️ Self-hosted GPU compute has high fixed cost
⚠️ Training costs recur as team turns over

Important caveats

Token costs are estimates based on average task complexity and frequency — actual usage will vary significantly. Salesforce add-on pricing (Agentforce, Einstein) changes frequently; always validate with Salesforce AE. FTE savings represent effort reduction, not headcount reduction. All figures should be validated against actual contract rates and tool agreements before client presentation.
